## ğŸ¯ Farming Strategies

### **Early Game Focus (Zones 1-3):**
Farm iron and fiber consistently - these are used in 80% of recipes

### **Mid Game Goals (Zones 4-6):**
- **Crystal hunting** in Zone 4 for magical gear
- **Silver grinding** in Zone 6 for jewelry
- **Fire/Ice crystals** for elemental weapons

### **Late Game Grind (Zones 7-9):**
- **Mithril ore** farming in Zone 7 (10-20 runs for one piece)
- **Dragon scale** grinding in Zone 9 (VERY rare, plan multiple runs)
- **Moonstone/Gemstone** collection for legendary rings

### **End Game Challenge (Zone 10):**
- **Orichalcum** for ultimate gear
- **Soul Crystal** for necrotic weapons
- **Infernal materials** for demon-themed builds

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Stockpile Common Materials** - You'll always need iron and fiber
2. **Save Legendary Materials** - Don't craft impulsively with dragon scales
3. **Specialize Your Farming** - Focus on one zone for specific materials
4. **Check Boss Weaknesses** - Farm the right weapon type before boss fights
5. **Material Trading** - Some NPC materials can only be obtained through tavern trades
6. **Rings First** - Rings give huge bonuses for relatively low material cost
7. **Plan Your Builds** - Know what you want to craft before farming
